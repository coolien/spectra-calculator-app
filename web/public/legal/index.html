We use access controls, row-level database security and encrypted HTTPS connections. No online service is risk-free, so keep your device and email account secure and avoid entering unnecessary sensitive information.

6. Access, export and delete your data

Delete local app data

Clear the stored data for calculatorapp.spectramsia.com using your browser's site settings. This removes the app data, preferences and consent record held by that browser and may sign you out. Browser menu names differ by device.

Export your app data

Open Settings, then Account & cloud sync, and select Export my Spectra data.

Delete the cloud backup

While signed in, open Settings, then Account & cloud sync, and select Delete cloud backup. This deletes the Spectra app snapshot, profile and cloud-consent records and signs you out. Local data on that device remains until you delete it separately.

Delete the complete account or make a privacy request

Email sales@spectramsia.com from the address used for Spectra. State whether you want access, correction, cloud-data deletion or complete authentication-account deletion. We may need to verify that the email account belongs to you. Please do not send passwords, OTPs, identity documents or financial records by email unless we specifically and securely request what is legally necessary.
