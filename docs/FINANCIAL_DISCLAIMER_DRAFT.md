# Financial Disclaimer Draft

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Malaysia Loan & Finance Planner provides estimates only.

The app does not offer, arrange, broker, underwrite, compare, sell, or approve loans. It does not collect loan applications, identity documents, collateral documents, or official financing documents.

All calculator results depend on user-entered values and editable assumptions. Actual figures can vary by bank, product, promotion, statement cycle, legal invoice, valuer invoice, tax treatment, PTPTN account status, government policy, and borrower profile.

Users should verify figures with the relevant bank, lawyer, valuer, developer, real estate agent, PTPTN, LHDN, KWSP, PERKESO, or other official authority before making decisions.
