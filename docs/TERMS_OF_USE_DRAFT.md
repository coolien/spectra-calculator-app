# Terms Of Use Draft

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Malaysia Loan & Finance Planner is provided as a planning and education tool.

## Educational Use

The app provides calculators and estimates for personal finance planning. It is not a bank, lender, broker, financial adviser, lawyer, tax adviser, valuer, real estate agency, or government agency.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for entering accurate values and verifying results with banks, lawyers, valuers, PTPTN, LHDN, KWSP, PERKESO, or other relevant parties before making financial, legal, tax, or property decisions.

## No Approval Guarantee

The app does not guarantee loan approval, affordability, rates, fees, exemptions, valuation outcomes, legal outcomes, or tax treatment.

## Permitted Use

Users may use the app for personal planning, comparison, and education. Users must not misuse the app, interfere with app operation, or use outputs to misrepresent financial ability to any third party.

## Changes

Calculator logic, assumptions, features, and legal text may change as the app develops and as Malaysia rules or product behavior change.
